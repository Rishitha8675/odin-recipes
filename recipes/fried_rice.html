VEGETABLE FRIED RICE

Description

A flavorful stir-fried rice dish packed with vibrant vegetables and aromatic spices.

Ingrediants

  • Cooked Rice
  • Carrots (1 medium, diced)
  • Green beans (a handful, finely chopped)
  • Bell peppers (½ cup, diced – any color)
  • Cabbage (½ cup, shredded)
  • Green peas (¼ cup, optional)
  • Spring onions (2-3, chopped; reserve greens for garnish)
  • Garlic (2-3 cloves, minced)
  • Ginger (1 tsp, finely chopped or grated)
  • Green chilies (1-2, finely chopped, optional for spice)
  • Soy Sauce (1-2 tbsp)
  • Vinegar (1 tsp)
  • Salt
  • Pepper Powder (½ tsp or to taste)
  • Chili Sauce (½ tsp, for heat)
  • Tomato Ketchup (1-2 tsp, for a slight sweetness)
  • Cooking Oil (2-3 tbsp)
  • Butter (optional, for added richness)
  • Chopped spring onion greens

Procedure:

  1. Prepare and cool 2 cups of rice (use leftover rice for best results).
  2. Dice carrots, beans, bell peppers, cabbage, and spring onions.
  3. Heat 2-3 tbsp oil in a wok/pan on high flame.
  4. Fry garlic, ginger, and green chilies until fragrant.
  5. Add chopped vegetables and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes (keep them crunchy).
  6. Mix in soy sauce, vinegar, and optional chili sauce/ketchup.
  7. Combine rice with the veggies; season with salt and pepper.
  8. Sprinkle spring onion greens.
  9. Enjoy your fried rice!
