BIRYANI


Description

A rich and aromatic layered rice dish cooked with meat, spices, and herbs.

Ingrediants

  • Chicken
  • Basmati rice
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Curd
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Green chillies
  • Fresh Coriander Leaves
  • Fresh Mint Leaves
  • Ghee
  • Oil
  • Water

Procedure

  1. Combine chicken with yogurt, spices, ginger-garlic paste, and let it rest for at least 30 minutes
  2. Parboil the rice with whole spices until it is 70% cooked
  3. Caramelize sliced onions in ghee/oil.
  4. Saut√© marinated chicken with tomatoes and herbs until cooked.
  5. Layer rice and chicken alternately, topping with fried onions, coriander, mint, and saffron milk
  6. Cover and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes to infuse flavors.
