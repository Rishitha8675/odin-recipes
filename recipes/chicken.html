Chilli chicken

Description

Chilli Chicken is a popular Indo-Chinese dish made with crispy fried chicken tossed in a spicy, tangy, and flavorful sauce.

Ingredients

  • Boneless Chicken (cubes)
  • Cornflour
  • All-purpose Flour
  • Egg
  • Soy Sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Oil
  • Garlic (finely chopped)
  • Ginger (finely chopped)
  • Green Chilies (sliced)
  • Onion (cubed)
  • Capsicum/Bell Peppers (cubed)
  • Soy Sauce
  • Chili Sauce
  • Tomato Ketchup
  • Vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Cornflour Slurry (cornflour + water)
  • Spring Onions (for garnish)

Procedure

  1. Mix chicken with cornflour, all-purpose flour, egg, soy sauce, vinegar, ginger-garlic paste, salt, and pepper. Let it rest for 20-30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and deep-fry the marinated chicken until golden brown. Remove and set aside.
  3. Heat 2-3 tbsp oil in a wok or pan. Add chopped garlic, ginger, and green chilies; saut√© until aromatic.
  4. Add cubed onions and bell peppers; stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Mix in soy sauce, chili sauce, tomato ketchup, vinegar, sugar, and salt. Stir well to combine the flavors.
  6. Add cornflour slurry and cook until the sauce thickens.
  7. Add the fried chicken pieces and toss them in the sauce to coat evenly.
  8. Sprinkle spring onions on top. Serve hot as a starter or with fried rice/noodles.
